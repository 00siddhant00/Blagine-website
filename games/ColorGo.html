About

Perfectly time your taps through same-colored obstacles. Color Go is a mobile game that is highly interactive and engaging. Simple but addictive gameplay will keep you entertained as you compete with players worldwide.

Features

  • Tap, Tap, Tap to get the ball past every obstacle.
  • Follow the color pattern to cross barriers.
  • Timing and Patience are the keys to victory.
  • Earn diamonds to unlock new skins.
  • Infinity Gameplay mode.

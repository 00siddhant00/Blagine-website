The Loop
  • Invest: Buy run time. Costs and gains scale exponentially.
  • Harvest: Link crystals for massive profits. Longer chains = higher multipliers.
  • Panic: Chains have weight. Stretch them too far and they snap. Fail to cash in before 0.00s and you lose it all.
  • Upgrade: Spend "Zek" on the Constellation Tree for permanent abilities and greed modifiers.
Features
  • Tactile Physics: Crisp, snappy chain mechanics rewarding precision. No floaty movement.
  • High Stakes Economy: A "Pay-to-Play" cycle. Wager earnings to fund the next run. Profit or crash.
